What kind of knowledge do I need to know to use the system?

Nothing specific, once the system has a frindly interface and very intuitive to make your job easier. Besides, you'll always have a help window on the right side of the screen.

Who is responsible for sending the messages I bought?

The messages are sent by the company that contracts the service. All submissions can be made from the web tool provided.

What kind of messages can I send?

It is possible to send informative messages, general announcements, reminders, endomarketing actions, collections and etc.

Is the sender that appears in the SMS sent by the tool a long number or a short number?

Since Movile is a broker homologated in all Brazilian operators, we only work with short codes.

Is it possible to hire a postpaid model?

Yes, it is possible. For this you need to contacto us through the email messaging@movile.com.

I lost my password to access the system, how do I recover it?

To recover your password, go to the login field and click on the "Retrieve password" link and enter your email, then a message will automatically be sent to the e-mail registered on the site describing the procedure to recover your access.

It is possible to send a bulk messages? How do I do?

Yes, you can send large batches. Simply create a list with the recipients' phones using a CSV or TXT format file by typing each of the phone lines in addition to the header.

Is it possible to replace the sender's number with my company name?

Currently this option is not available by the operators in Brazil, thus remaining a short number of up to 5 digits as sender.

Is it possible to integrate with my system?

Yes, it is possible. See our API documentation.
